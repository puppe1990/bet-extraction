# Chrome Web Store Copy

## Extension Name

BankrollKit Capture

## Short Description

Capture sportsbook bet drafts from the active tab and sync them into your BankrollKit account.

## Detailed Description

BankrollKit Capture connects your browser to BankrollKit so you can turn a sportsbook page into a clean bet draft without copying fields by hand.

What it does:

- sign in to your BankrollKit account from the extension popup
- capture bookmaker, event, market, selection, odds and stake from the active page
- send the draft to BankrollKit for review and normalization
- save the draft into your betting ledger
- show a compact snapshot of balance, plan and recent activity

Why it exists:

- reduce manual entry when logging bets
- keep records cleaner before review and settlement
- make sportsbook capture operational instead of ad hoc

Current support:

- best-effort parser for Bet365
- generic fallback capture for unsupported layouts

Important notes:

- capture quality depends on the structure of the sportsbook page
- unsupported pages may require manual review before saving
- a BankrollKit account is required

## Single Purpose Statement

BankrollKit Capture helps BankrollKit users capture bet drafts from the current sportsbook page and sync them to their BankrollKit account.
